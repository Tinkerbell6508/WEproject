About Herb & Hearth

We believe recipes are memories made visible. Herb & Hearth is a lightweight recipe sharing app designed to make saving and sharing home-cooked meals simple and beautiful.

This demo app uses Node.js, Express and MongoDB Atlas. Frontend uses plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
